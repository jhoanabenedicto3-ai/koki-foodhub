# DISCOUNT DROPDOWN - QUICK REFERENCE

## Location in App
**Koki's Foodhub Dashboard** → **Right Panel (Transaction Cart)** → **Discount (Optional) Section**

## Discount Options & Values

| Option | Discount % | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| No Discount | 0% | Regular customers |
| Senior Citizen | 20% | Senior citizens (60+ years old) |
| PWD | 20% | People with disabilities |
| Student | 5% | Students with valid ID |

## How to Use

### For Cashiers/Staff:
1. Add products to cart as usual
2. Scroll to **"Discount (Optional)"** section in the Transaction Cart
3. Click the dropdown and select appropriate discount:
   - "No Discount (0%)" - for regular payment
   - "Senior Citizen – 20%" - for senior customers
   - "PWD – 20%" - for PWD customers
   - "Student – 5%" - for students
4. The system automatically updates:
   - Discount label (e.g., "Senior Citizen (20%)")
   - Discount amount in pesos (e.g., "₱100.00")
   - Final TOTAL (Subtotal - Discount Amount)
5. Enter amount paid
6. System calculates change based on the discounted total
7. Click "PRINT RECEIPT" to complete the transaction

### Display Updates:
- **Subtotal**: ₱500.00 (sum of all items)
- **Discount Label**: Shows selected discount type
- **Discount Amount**: Shows peso value of discount (only shown if discount > 0)
- **TOTAL**: Shows final amount customer must pay
- **Change**: Automatically calculated from discounted total

## Calculation Examples

### Example 1: Senior Citizen (20% discount)
```
Subtotal:        ₱500.00
Discount (20%):  -₱100.00  ← Automatically calculated
TOTAL:           ₱400.00   ← Customer pays this amount

Amount Paid:     ₱500.00
Change:          ₱100.00   ← Automatically calculated
```

### Example 2: Student (5% discount)
```
Subtotal:        ₱300.00
Discount (5%):   -₱15.00   ← Automatically calculated
TOTAL:           ₱285.00   ← Customer pays this amount

Amount Paid:     ₱300.00
Change:          ₱15.00    ← Automatically calculated
```

### Example 3: No Discount (0%)
```
Subtotal:        ₱250.00
Discount (0%):   ₱0.00     ← No discount applied
TOTAL:           ₱250.00   ← Customer pays this amount

Amount Paid:     ₱250.00
Change:          ₱0.00     ← Automatically calculated
```

## Key Features

✓ **Real-time Updates**: Changes instantly when discount is selected
✓ **Automatic Calculation**: No manual math needed, system calculates everything
✓ **One Discount Only**: Can't combine discounts
✓ **Clear Display**: Shows discount label, amount, and final total
✓ **Receipt Included**: Discount is printed on the receipt
✓ **Auto-Reset**: Resets to "No Discount" after checkout

## Important Notes

- Always verify the customer qualifies for the discount before selecting it
- Senior Citizen and PWD discounts both give 20% off
- Student discount gives only 5% off
- The change calculation automatically includes the discount
- Discounts apply to the entire order total, not individual items

## Support
